# Merge & Mischief
## Status
In development, not ready for beta testing.

## Intent
The intent of this mod is to add interesting yet vanilla friendly content to Might and Magic Merge.

- Designed to be lore- and balance-friendly. No new skills, balance changes, or items.
- Adds flavorful side content that enriches without disrupting the main storylines.
- Some quests may leave lasting changes in the game world, but none should interfere with the main storyline, the nostalgia induced by Rodril’s Merge, the refined nostalgic experience of Cthscr's Revamp, or the novel feel of MAW.

## Questlines
### The Goblins Strikes Back
#### Difficulty
Easier than the quest Damsel in Distress located in the Silver Helm Outpost at Misty Island.

#### Starting map
New Sorpigal (Enroth, MM6).

#### Prerequisite
Quest: Find the combination to the vault door in Goblinwatch, given by Janice in Town Hall in New Sorpigal.

#### Start 
This quest becomes available after completing the standard Goblinwatch quest in New Sorpigal.

Hint: After finishing the original Goblinwatch quest, Janice in the Town Hall will provide a clue that guides you toward Urok, the quest giver.
  
Quest giver: Urok, the former resident of the apartment above Goblinwatch. Have now taken refuge in Dorf's house behind Goblinwatch.

### The Spymaster's Request

#### Starting map
Blackshire (Enroth, MM6).

#### Start
Quest giver: Carter, a new NPC residing in the house just east of the Alchemist Store, "Ty's Trinkets", and the Smuggler's Guild

#### Difficulty
Between Blackshire and Kriegspire in terms of difficulty

### The Orphanage
**Warning**, this quest might be too dark for some.

#### Starting map
Free haven (Enroth, MM6).

#### Start
Quest giver: Steve, a new NPC who resides in a small house near the docks on the eastern side of town.

#### Difficulty
Difficulty is easier than Free Haven.

### Return of the Prince of Thieves
#### Starting map
Free Haven (Enroth, MM6).

#### Prerequisite
Quest: The Prince of Thieves, given by baron Anthony Stones in Frozen Highlands

#### Start
Quest giver: Morgan the Disguiser, a new NPC who resides with Ivan Magyar in the same building as the Smuggler's Guild. On the north eastern side of Free haven.

#### Difficulty
The difficulty is about the same as Frozen Highlands.

## Important changes
### Free Haven
The role of Expert Disarm teacher has been given to Sergio Carrington. Sergio resides in the same building as the previous Expert Disarm teacher, but not behind the same door. He is now found behind the same door as the Free Haven Sewer entrance.
