# Merge & Mischief

## The Goblins Strikes Back
### Difficulty
Easier than the quest Damsel in Distress located in the Silver Helm Outpost at Misty Island.

### Map
New Sorpigal (Enroth, MM6).

### Prerequisite
Quest: Find the combination to the vault door in Goblinwatch, given by Janice in Town Hall in New Sorpigal.

### Start 
This quest becomes available after completing the standard Goblinwatch quest in New Sorpigal.

- Quest Giver: Urok, a former resident of the apartment above Goblinwatch, now taking refuge in Dorf's house behind Goblinwatch.

- Hint: After finishing the original Goblinwatch quest, Janice in the Town Hall will eventually provide a clue to guide you toward Urok.

## Intent
The intent of this mod is to add interesting yet vanilla friendly content to Might and Magic Merge.

- Designed to be lore- and balance-friendly. No new skills, balance changes, or items.
- Adds flavorful side content that enriches without disrupting the main storylines.
- Some quests may leave lasting changes in the game world, but none should interfere with the main storyline, the nostalgia induced by Rodril’s Merge, the refined nostalgic experience of Cthscr's Revamp, or the novel feel of MAW.
